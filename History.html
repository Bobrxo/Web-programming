1.7million years ago

Yunnan is a province in China, with its capital city being Kunming. Located in the southwestern part of China, Yunnan's history can be traced back to the Yuanmou Man, who lived around 1.7 million years ago. They were among the earliest hominins to have walked upright in Asia.

B.C.300

In the 3rd century BC, the first kingdom emerged in Yunnan. Later, it was unified during the Qin Dynasty. Since then, Yunnan has been an important transportation hub for exchanges between China and other countries to the south.

20 Century

In the early 20th century, China was going through one of its most tumultuous periods. Yunnan, as the only land route connecting to foreign territories, played a crucial role during World War II.

Now

Today, Yunnan is one of China's renowned tourist destinations. Due to its unique climate, people from other regions in China choose to visit Yunnan during the summer and winter. It offers pleasant temperatures, neither too cold nor too hot. Additionally, the region boasts a wide variety of delicious cuisine and a distinctive cultural charm.
